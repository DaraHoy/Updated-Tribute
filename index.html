Warrior Princess of Themyscira

Diana is an Amazonian warrior princess and superhero known as Wonder Woman. By day, she works as an antiquities dealer at the Louvre Museum as Diana Prince. She is the daughter of the Amazons' Queen Hippolyta and Olympian Gods' King Zeus

Powers & Abilities

  • Superhuman strength, speed, durability, and longevity
  • Skilled in warfare
  • Utilizes Lasso of Truth, indestructible bracelets
